* EVALUATION LEVEL EXAMPLE QUESTIONS *

01. Evaluate the effectiveness of the New Deal policies in addressing the economic challenges of the Great Depression. Discuss the short-term and long-term impacts on American society.
02. Critically assess the portrayal of gender roles in Virginia Woolf's "A Room of One's Own" and its relevance to contemporary feminist discourse.
03. Evaluate the ethical implications of genetic engineering in agriculture. Discuss the potential benefits and risks, considering both environmental and societal perspectives.
04. Analyze the effectiveness of the United Nations in maintaining international peace and security. Evaluate specific interventions and their outcomes over the past two decades.
05. Evaluate the impact of globalization on income inequality within and between countries. Consider both economic data and social consequences in your analysis.
06. Assess the validity and reliability of cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) as a treatment for anxiety disorders. Compare it with other therapeutic approaches.
07. Evaluate the role of social media in shaping public opinion and political discourse. Discuss its potential to both democratize information and spread misinformation.
08. Assess the effectiveness of international climate agreements, such as the Paris Agreement, in mitigating climate change. Discuss their strengths, weaknesses, and potential areas for improvement.
09. Critically evaluate the ethical arguments for and against euthanasia. Consider perspectives from utilitarianism, deontology, and virtue ethics.
10. Evaluate the impact of standardized testing on educational quality and equity. Discuss the benefits and drawbacks, and propose potential alternatives.
