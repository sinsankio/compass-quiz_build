* APPLICATION LEVEL EXAMPLE QUESTIONS *

01. Explain how the principles of the Enlightenment influenced the development of modern democratic governments. Use examples from at least two different countries to support your response.
02. Discuss how the process of natural selection operates in the wild, and apply this concept to explain the development of antibiotic resistance in bacteria.
03. Analyze the use of symbolism in one of William Faulkner's novels and explain how it contributes to the overall themes and messages of the work.
04. Describe how the principles of electromagnetism are applied in the design and functioning of electric motors. Provide specific examples of where these motors are used in everyday life.
05. Discuss the application of the separation of powers in the United States government. Explain how this principle helps to prevent the abuse of power and provide specific examples.
06. Explain how the concept of comparative advantage can be used to understand international trade patterns. Use specific countries and industries to illustrate your points.
07. Discuss how cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) can be applied to treat anxiety disorders. Provide examples of techniques used in CBT and how they help patients manage their symptoms.
08. Analyze the impact of social media on interpersonal relationships and community building. Apply sociological theories to explain both positive and negative effects.
09. Explain how geographic information systems (GIS) can be used to manage natural disasters. Provide examples of specific disasters and how GIS helped in their management.
10. Discuss how the principles of object-oriented programming (OOP) can be applied to develop a software application for managing a library system. Include examples of classes, objects, and methods that would be used.
